FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a tender bid?
Login to your contractor dashboard, browse the available tenders, select the one you're interested in, and click the "Submit Bid" button. Follow the prompts to upload your technical and financial proposals.
Can I edit my submitted bid?
Yes, you can edit your submitted bid as long as the tender deadline has not passed. Go to "My Bids" in your dashboard to find and update your submission.
What documents are required for registration?
Typically, you will need your company registration certificate, tax clearance, and relevant professional licenses specific to your industry specialization.
How will I know if my bid was successful?
You will receive an instant notification in your dashboard and via email once the administrator has reviewed and made a decision on the bids for that tender.
